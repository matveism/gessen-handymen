About GESSEN

Professional Handyman Services You Can Rely On

At GESSEN, we deliver more than just repairs — we provide complete, professional solutions to make your home work better, look better, and feel better.

We're a licensed, bonded, and insured contractor with years of hands-on experience in:

  • Home Repairs – Fixing everything from leaky faucets to squeaky doors
  • Painting & Drywall – Interior and exterior painting, drywall repair
  • Furniture Assembly – Professional assembly of all furniture types
  • Light Fixture Installation – Safe and proper installation of lighting
  • General Maintenance – Routine home maintenance and repairs

From small fixes to larger projects — we show up on time, work clean, and get it done right.
